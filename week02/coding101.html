Donut Recipe:

  1. Preheat the oven to 150 C / gas mark 2.
  2. Add the vanilla, mix, then add the flour and mix well.
  3. Add butter and sugar in a bowl and mix well until light and fluffy.
  4. Roll out to about 5mm. Cut into shapes.
  5. Bake for 25 minutes or until golden brown.
